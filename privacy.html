Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-07-21 · Cedarstone Ventures LLC

Cedarstone Ventures LLC ("Cedarstone," "we") builds money and tax tools for people who rent out property. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. We keep it short because we collect very little.

What we collect

  • Your email address, only if you choose to give it to us (for example, to receive the free tax checklist).
  • Basic, privacy-respecting analytics: aggregate page visits, with no cross-site tracking and no sale of personal data.

How we use it

  • To send you the resource you requested and occasional, genuinely useful rental-money tips.
  • To understand, in aggregate, which content is helpful so we can make better tools.

What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not send spam. Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe, and you can ask us to delete your data at any time.

Your choices

Unsubscribe from any email, or email info@cedarstonellc.com to access or delete the information we hold about you.

Contact

Questions? info@cedarstonellc.com. If this policy changes, we'll update the effective date above.
